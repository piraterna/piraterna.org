About Piraterna

Piraterna is a global collective focused on creating freedom-oriented software. Though we started in Sweden, our community includes people from all over the world who believe in using technology to empower individuals.

Founded on November 26, 2024, Piraterna is dedicated to creating open-source software that’s accessible and transparent. We want our software to be free for everyone to use, modify, and distribute. By joining Piraterna, you’re supporting a movement that makes technology work for people.

Our Mission and Values

At Piraterna, our mission is to build software that belongs to everyone. Here’s what we value:

  • Freedom: Our software is free as in freedom. You can use, modify, and share it however you like.
  • Transparency: We’re open about how we develop our software. Anyone can see our code and contribute.
  • Privacy: We respect your privacy. We don’t track you or collect unnecessary data.
  • Collaboration: We believe in working together to improve our software. Everyone’s contributions are welcome.

Becoming a Pirat

Note: Pirat means Pirate in Swedish. It's our term for a member of the community.

Piraterna is a non-profit organization that relies on the generosity of our community to fund the maintenance of our servers, tools, and infrastructure. Your support helps us continue building and offering free software to everyone. The best part? You decide how much to contribute – there’s no fixed amount.

All donations go directly into supporting Piraterna’s work. By becoming a Pirat, you’re helping ensure the long-term sustainability of our projects. However, you don’t need to pay anything to use or contribute to our software – membership is simply a way to support our mission.

Note: We don’t have an official way to become a Pirat, yet. But you can still join by contacting us at @fwpirate:matrix.org. We’ll start a conversation and get you involved!
